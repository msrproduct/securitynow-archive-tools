### ‚ùå Mistake #5: GRC Regex HTML Entity
**Frequency:** 4 times, 8+ hours debugging  
**Problem:** GRC uses `&#160;` (non-breaking space), NOT pipes or regular spaces

**‚úÖ Correct Regex:**
```powershell
'Episode&#160;(\d{1,4})&#160;,&#160;(\d{1,2})'
```

**‚ùå Wrong patterns:**
```powershell

'Episode\s+(\d+)\s+,(\d+)'      # Fails on &#160;
```
